RO.ME "3 DREAMS OF BLACK"

RO.ME is an interactive experience film built in WebGL. Users are able to move around steer themselves throughout the film; this means that the user moves their cursor they're able to see various visual effects and filters/effects are added in the place where the cursor was. It is a music video made for the web browser - it has both 2D and 3D visuals. Although this project was done more than a decade ago it is very interesting to you see how far browser-based visuals have advanced. Now we have 360 interactive videos and images in youtube and google.

Technology: WebGL, WebGL Inspector, JavaScript, HTML5, Three.js 3D library, Google Chrome Experiments
